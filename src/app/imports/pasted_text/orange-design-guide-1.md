🔶 JOIN OUR ECOSYSTEM — FULL ORANGE VERSION
⚠️ Key Rule

Full orange can easily look cheap.
So we balance it with:

depth (gradients)
contrast (dark cards OR inverted cards)
controlled typography
✅ 1. BACKGROUND (NOT FLAT ORANGE)

Use layered orange:

background:
radial-gradient(
  circle at 50% 20%,
  rgba(255,255,255,0.08),
  transparent 60%
),
linear-gradient(
  135deg,
  #FF6A3D 0%,
  #FF7E4F 40%,
  #FF5A2E 100%
);

👉 Gives richness instead of flat fill

✅ 2. TYPOGRAPHY (VERY IMPORTANT)
Option A (Recommended – Premium Contrast)

Heading:

color: #FFFFFF;

Subtext:

color: rgba(255,255,255,0.8);

👉 Clean, high contrast, readable

✅ 3. CARDS (NOW INVERTED SYSTEM)

You have 2 strong options:

🔥 OPTION 1 (BEST — HIGH CONTRAST)

👉 Keep cards dark navy

background: #0F172A;

Why this works:

Orange = energy
Dark cards = stability
Perfect contrast
🔥 OPTION 2 (MORE EXPERIMENTAL — FULL ORANGE SYSTEM)

👉 Light cards on orange:

background: rgba(255,255,255,0.95);

Hover:

background: #FFFFFF;
transform: translateY(-6px);

👉 I strongly recommend Option 1 (dark cards) for your platform

✅ 4. CARD HOVER (IMPORTANT)
hover:
transform: translateY(-8px);

box-shadow:
0 25px 60px rgba(0,0,0,0.25),
0 0 30px rgba(255,255,255,0.2);

👉 White glow instead of orange → avoids overload

✅ 5. CTA BUTTON (INVERT IT)

Since background is orange:

background: #FFFFFF;
color: #FF6A3D;

Hover:

background: #FFF7F4;
transform: translateY(-2px);

👉 This becomes your visual break

✅ 6. STATS SECTION (IMPORTANT BALANCE)

Keep neutral:

background: #FFFFFF;

Text:

#1F2937 (dark)

👉 This prevents orange fatigue

🏗️ FOOTER — SILHOUETTE TOP (LIKE REFERENCE, BUT RELEVANT)

Now the fun part.

🔥 1. REMOVE STRAIGHT TOP EDGE

Instead of flat line → use custom silhouette shape

🔥 2. SILHOUETTE CONCEPT (CONSTRUCTION THEME)

Instead of shoes/city → use:

Cranes 🏗️
Buildings 🏢
Scaffolding
Pipes / materials
Factory outlines
🔧 How to design in Figma:
Create vector path (wave-like base)
Add silhouette shapes on top:
cranes
buildings
Merge into one shape
🎨 Color:
fill: #050816;

or slightly lighter:

#0A0F1C
🔥 3. ADD DEPTH TO SILHOUETTE

Add slight shadow:

drop shadow:
0 -10px 30px rgba(0,0,0,0.4);

👉 Makes it feel layered, not pasted

🔥 4. FOOTER BACKGROUND (KEEP YOUR GLOW)
background:
radial-gradient(
  circle at 70% 120%,
  rgba(255,106,61,0.35),
  transparent 45%
),
radial-gradient(
  circle at 30% 120%,
  rgba(255,106,61,0.25),
  transparent 50%
),
linear-gradient(
  to bottom,
  #04070F,
  #02040A
);
🔥 5. SILHOUETTE SEPARATION (IMPORTANT)

Add subtle line:

border-bottom: 1px solid rgba(255,255,255,0.05);
